MOBILE DEVELOPMENT

Mobile engineers are the engineers who develop and build software applications for smartphones and other mobile devices, with some specializing in Android applications, iOS apps, or Windows Mobile apps. Beyond developing mobile apps, mobile engineers are also the ones responsible for troubleshooting problems and ensuring the app performs according to the likes and specifications of stakeholders.

MOBILE DEVELOPMENT SKILLS
  • Working together with the client, UX designer, and other engineers to establish a common vision.
  • Designing code architecture.
  • Implementing design screens, buttons, scrollbars, and other navigational elements.
  • Implementation, debugging, and testing mobile apps.
  • Liaising with other developers(backend) to ensure correct implementation.
